    Commands with arguments

    What What
    -nargs=0 0 arguments, default
    -nargs=1 1 argument, includes spaces
    -nargs=? 0 or 1 argument
    -nargs=* 0+ arguments, space separated
    -nargs=+ 1+ arguments, space reparated
